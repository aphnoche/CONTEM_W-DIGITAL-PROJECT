# CONTEM_W Digital Project — Human Rights Violations

An interactive, web-based exploration of severe human rights violations worldwide. This project delves into three major contemporary crises, offering in-depth articles, interactive mind maps, and interactive simulations to educate users on the causes, impacts, and potential paths to justice.


This website was made with Agentic coding, and researched manually.

**Live Site:** [https://aphnoche.github.io/CONTEM_W-DIGITAL-PROJECT](https://aphnoche.github.io/CONTEM_W-DIGITAL-PROJECT)  
**Repository:** [https://github.com/aphnoche/CONTEM_W-DIGITAL-PROJECT](https://github.com/aphnoche/CONTEM_W-DIGITAL-PROJECT)

## 📖 Project Overview

This digital project focuses on three primary areas of global injustice:

1. **Extrajudicial Killings (War on Drugs) — Philippines** An analysis of the state-sponsored violence during the Duterte administration, the statistical devastation on the urban poor, high-profile case studies, and global diplomatic repercussions.
2. **The Gaza Crisis and the Regional Humanitarian Collapse** An exploration of the unprecedented escalation of the Israel-Hamas conflict, detailing the total blockade, infrastructure eradication, mass displacement, and the ripple effects across the Middle East.
3. **ICE Mass Detention and the Criminalization of Immigrants — United States** A look into the multibillion-dollar punitive immigration system, highlighting the inhumane conditions of privatized facilities, severe economic contractions, and the diplomatic strain on Latin American relations.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Interactive Mind Maps:** Visual breakdowns of the complex factors driving each crisis.
- **In-Depth Articles:** Comprehensive readings analyzing the historical context and human toll of each issue.
- **Global Impact Map:** An interactive map powered by Leaflet.js that allows users to hover over regions to see how these violations affect the world.
- **Interactive Learning Tools:**
  - *Reality Simulator:* Alter key variables in historical timelines to see how different policy decisions could have fundamentally reconstructed these crises.
  - *Path to Justice Puzzle:* A drag-and-drop interactive module challenging users to construct the steps toward accountability and peace.
  - *Topic-Specific Games & Visualizers:* EJK Info Game, Gaza Visualizer, and ICE Info Game.
- **Immersive Experience:** Includes toggleable background music and a fully responsive, modern UI.

## ✊ Our Stand (Advocacy Statement)

> "Across all subtopics—state violence in the Philippines, asymmetrical warfare in Gaza, and the carceral immigration system in the United States—war and state-sanctioned violence are core drivers of human rights violations. A clear pattern of power inequality emerges: those in positions of authority declare and execute violence, while civilians and the marginalized bear the consequences. Citizens lose their rights to live peacefully and have basic necessities met. **We stand against wars and all acts of human rights violation, including discrimination, inequality, and oppression.**"
