The Why

Behind every statistic
is a human life.

Numbers can be numbing. Statistics strip away humanity. This project exists to close the distance between abstract data and the faces behind it.

We invite you not as a bystander, but as a witness — to transform awareness into agency, and empathy into a demand for accountability.

Philippine Drug War

Right to life, due process, and freedom from torture

  • ICCPR Articles 6 and 14 on life and fair trial
  • Convention Against Torture protections
  • UN OHCHR and UN Human Rights Council findings
ICCPR Overview Convention Against Torture

Human Rights Violations committed

  • Extrajudicial killings
  • Torture
  • Denial of due process

Cases people recognize

  • Kian Delos Santos shooting
  • Florjohn Cruz raid
  • Tokhang operations and nanlaban claims

Gaza Crisis

Protection of civilians and humanitarian access

  • International humanitarian law and Geneva Conventions
  • UN OCHA protection of civilians standards
  • UN OHCHR reporting on rights violations
Geneva Conventions OCHA Protection of Civilians

Human Rights Violations committed

  • Collective punishment
  • Indiscriminate attacks
  • Starvation siege tactics

Cases people recognize

  • Total blockade and aid restrictions
  • Hospital strikes and medical collapse
  • Mass displacement into shrinking safe zones

ICE Mass Detention

  • ICCPR safeguards against arbitrary detention

  • Convention Against Torture safeguards
