The Why

Behind every statistic
is a human life.

Numbers can be numbing. Statistics strip away humanity. This project exists to close the distance between abstract data and the faces behind it.

We invite you not as a bystander, but as a witness — to transform awareness into agency, and empathy into a demand for accountability.
